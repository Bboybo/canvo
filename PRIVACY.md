# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

Canvo ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how data is handled.

## Data Collection

**Canvo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.**

- All conversations, files, and agent memory are stored locally on your device
- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting is sent to us
- No user accounts are required

## AI Provider Communication

When you use an AI provider (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini), your prompts are sent directly from your device to the provider's API. Canvo does not proxy, intercept, or store these communications.

- **API keys** are stored locally in your device's secure storage
- **Google OAuth tokens** (for Gemini) are stored locally and refreshed on-device
- Each provider has its own privacy policy that governs how your data is handled

## Local Storage

All app data is stored on your device:
- Conversation history
- Agent memory and knowledge base
- Custom tools and scripts
- Heartbeat task schedules
- Files created in the Linux sandbox

Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
